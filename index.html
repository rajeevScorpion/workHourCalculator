You can only use 1.5 hrs surplus in a day! Since minimum work duration limit for a day is 7 hours.

Quick Actions

  1. Fill "in time" and press calculate to know what time can you leave!
  2. Fill "in time" and "out time" and press calculate to know total work hours and how much surplus or shortfall you have.
  3. Fill "in time" and "surplus/shortfall" then select "surplus or shortfall" from radio button and press calculate to know how early or late can you leave.
