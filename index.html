How to Use?

When can you leave for the day?
Fill "in time" and press calculate to know what time can you leave!
Calculate surplus or shortfall for the day?
Fill "in time" and "out time" and press calculate to know total work hours and how much surplus or shortfall you have.
Use your surplus or cover for shortfall.
Fill "in time" and "surplus/shortfall" then select "surplus or shortfall" from radio button and press calculate to know how early or late can you leave.
